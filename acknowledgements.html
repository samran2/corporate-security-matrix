Idea Developer

Sami Rantanen is recognized as the developer of the original idea.

The Corporate Security Matrix was shaped from the intent to create a practical, accessible and internationally useful operating model for corporate security. This page formally credits Sami Rantanen for that initiating idea and direction.

Project Direction

From concept to a public-use security instrument.

The project was not framed as a closed internal artifact. It was directed toward a broader mission: giving organizations a common structure for understanding threats, actors, controls and recovery decisions across physical, cyber, operational and continuity domains.

Why This Matters

The purpose is protection, clarity and shared resilience.

The matrix is intended to help companies anticipate serious risk earlier, organize mitigation more coherently and recover with less confusion. That public-protective purpose is central to the contribution being acknowledged here.

Open-Source Spirit

Built to be useful globally and improved collaboratively.

The initiative is aligned with open-source use, transparent source quality and practical reuse. It invites contributors, operators and security leaders to build on the work in a way that serves real organizations facing real disruption and harm.
