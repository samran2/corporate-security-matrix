# Fact Check

Verification date: March 31, 2026

This repository was fact-checked against official source domains before publication preparation.

## Verification method

1. Automated URL validation was run across `index.html`, `matrix-references.js`, `README.md` and `SOURCE-LIBRARY.md`.
2. High-risk references with edition numbers, revision numbers or redirecting canonical pages were manually checked against their official publication pages.
3. Titles in `matrix-references.js` and `SOURCE-LIBRARY.md` were updated where the repository wording was less precise than the official publication title.

## Automated result

- 44 external URLs validated successfully during the release check.
- Validation command: `python3 scripts/validate_project.py`

## Remaining cautions

- Official pages can change canonical URLs, publication metadata or navigation structure over time even when the document itself remains valid.
- Some WHO and IRIS pages use dynamic or redirected endpoints. The repository keeps stable official URLs, but maintainers should rerun validation before major releases.
- This repository is an original synthesis grounded in official sources. It does not reproduce or supersede the full normative content of the cited standards.
